PLAYER UNKNOWN'S BATTLE GROUNDS

Battlegrounds is a player versus player(PvP) action based first person shooter in which upto one hundred players fight in a battle royale , a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive. players can choose to enter the mach solo , or with a small team of upto four people . In either case the last person or team left alive wins the match.
